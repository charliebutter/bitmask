how to play

fill the main 4x4 grid with 0s and 1s to satisfy all five masks.

the basics

  • click cells in the main grid to toggle between 0 and 1
  • each mask below shows a pattern of blue highlighted cells
  • the numbers show your progress: [current count / target count]

satisfying a mask

  • look at the blue highlighted cells in the mask
  • count how many of those positions also have a 1 in your main grid
  • make that count equal the target number

example

if a mask shows "2/3" above it:

  • the target is 3, and you currently have 2 matches
  • you need 1 more cell where both the mask is blue AND your grid has a 1

solve the puzzle by making all five masks show matching numbers!
